Create a program with an accompanying menu to manage a list of students using a singly linked list (SLL) with the following operations. Note that the information of a student includes: Full Name, Student ID, Year of Birth, and Average Score.

Input the list of N students from the keyboard by adding to the beginning of the SLL and display the entered information.
Input the list of N students from the keyboard by adding to the end of the SLL and display the entered information.
Print out the list of students with an average score less than 5.
Delete students born before the year 2000 from the list.
Sort students in ascending order by average score.
Delete all students from the list, but before deleting, print out the information of the students to be removed from the list.
